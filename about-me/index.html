About Me & Your First-Year Journey!

Hi there, I'm Shravya N Bhat, a 2nd Year Information Science and Engineering student at NMAMIT. My first year was an incredibly enriching experience of learning, growth, and making unforgettable memories.

I was actively involved, serving as a Class Representative (CR), which honed my leadership and communication skills through coordinating with classmates and faculty. Beyond academics, I've boosted my skills with online courses and an AIC Internship, and I seized every opportunity to showcase my talents in singing and dancing.

My Advice for Your First Year:

  • Academics:Strive to avoid backlogs and maintain a good CGPA; it's achievable. If you face challenges, clear exams in the summer semester.
  • Mentorship:While a mentor is assigned, proactively seek guidance from knowledgeable lecturers, seniors, or industry experts to keep learning.
  • Well-being & Growth: Prioritize your physical and mental health, strategize, and explore interests to find what brings you satisfaction.

Making the Most of Your First Year:

Your first year is the best time to build a strong foundation. Embrace every moment, explore new horizons (both technical and cultural), and solidify your basics. Participate in college events, including the Techno-Cultural Fest - Incridea, and don't hesitate to volunteer; seniors are always ready to guide you. For a peaceful break, explore Sanmathi Park or B.C. Alva.

Networking, communication, and diverse skill development are key.
Engage with peers, seniors, and faculty. Make the most of this exciting chapter!
