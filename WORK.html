For First year B.TECH students(NMAMIT)

Semester-1

The Physics Cycle is an interdisciplinary program covering Calculus, Differential Equations, and MATLAB for mathematical and computational skills. It explores Quantum Computing and Modern Physics, complemented by Problem Solving through Programming and Basic Electronics. The curriculum also includes Fundamentals of Cyber Security, Technical English, and the Constitution of India.

Semester-2

The Chemistry Cycle is a multidisciplinary program covering Discrete Mathematics and Transform Techniques, Materials Chemistry for Computer Systems, and Applied Digital Logic Systems. It explores Introduction to Python Programming, complemented by Basic Electrical Engineering and Biology for Engineers. The curriculum also includes Engineering Visualization, and Environmental Studies and Sustainability.
